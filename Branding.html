Brand Name

Penta Collision is the name of our brand, which we derived from the team's identification. We chose this name based on the connotations we wanted the brand name to convey, and that are our mission and personalization.

Logo

We devised a scheme to associate the controls in a controller to our LOGO in order to represent our chosen content, which is gaming. We believed that this concept would assist us in delivering a friendly, modern, and fun look, leading to people looking forward to our content.

Our goal

Our mission in this brand is to provide our viewers with a platform where they can find both what they need and what they want for entertainment and learning at the same time.

Tagline

Our tagline "Get in the game with Penta Collision" is derived from our chosen brand name, which represents our team and content by combining the words "Penta," which means five, and "Collision," that represents the merging of our ideas. It also includes the chosen topic of our content, gaming, from the phrase "get in the game with..."

Intro Video

Our Intro Video comes from our chosen brand name "Penta Collision" we have chosen this intro because we want to deliver a friendly introduction. The Intro Video represents a typical but unique gaming intro because of its attention catching and recognizable scheme of its fun and dynamic colors.
